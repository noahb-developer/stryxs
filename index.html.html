Help & Getting Started

Everything you need to get the most out of Stryxs.

⌚ Exporting from Garmin
1
Go to connect.garmin.com on your computer.
2
Click Activities, then click any workout.
3
Click the ⚙️ gear icon in the top right.
4
Click Export to CSV.
5
Upload that file in the Analyze tab.
🍎 Exporting from Apple Watch
1
Open the Health app on your iPhone.
2
Tap your profile picture (top right) → Export All Health Data.
3
This exports a ZIP. For a simpler option, use a free app like HealthFit or RunGap to export individual workouts as CSV.
4
Upload the CSV in the Analyze tab.

Direct Apple Health integration coming soon.

⌚ Exporting from COROS
1
Go to trainingtoday.coros.com on your computer.
2
Click on any activity in your timeline.
3
Click the Export button and choose CSV.
4
Upload it in the Analyze tab.
📱 Exporting from Samsung Galaxy Watch
1
Open Samsung Health app on your phone.
2
Tap the menu (☰) → Settings → Download personal data.
3
Confirm and wait for the email with your data ZIP.
4
For individual workouts, third-party apps like Strava (auto-syncs Samsung) or FITWatch Sync can export CSV.

Direct Samsung Health integration coming soon.

🎯 Finding your LTHR

LTHR (Lactate Threshold Heart Rate) is the most important number for accurate zones.

1
Warm up for 15 minutes.
2
Do a 30-minute solo time trial at the hardest pace you can sustain.
3
Your average HR for the last 20 minutes is your LTHR.

Test run and bike separately. They differ by 5-10 bpm.

Frequently asked questions
What watches does Stryxs support?
Any watch that exports CSV, TCX, GPX, or FIT files — Garmin, Apple Watch, COROS, Polar, Suunto, Wahoo. Garmin is most common and best supported.
Will you add automatic syncing?
Yes — Strava integration is coming first (covers most watches since Strava auto-syncs them). Direct Garmin and Apple Health integration will follow.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your data is stored encrypted in our database. Only you can access it. We never share or sell training data.
How accurate are the analyses?
Based on published sports science: Friel LTHR zones, cardiac drift research, acute:chronic workload models. Accuracy depends on YOUR inputs — especially your LTHR. Test it properly.
What's the difference between Stryxs and Strava?
Strava is a social network for activities. Stryxs is a coaching analyzer. We turn data into coaching decisions. Strava doesn't.
What does it cost?
Free tier: unlimited analyses, 7 days of history. Pro ($15/mo): unlimited history, trend analysis, race planning, auto platform sync. Elite ($29/mo): Pro + injury risk scoring, nutrition periodization, race-day planning.
